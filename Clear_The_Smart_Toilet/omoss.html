About us

We in Clear are unique and effective in many ways. We differentiate ourselves from other toilet services by providing an environmentally friendly service. The whole crew started this project to make the world a better place to live in. Such a simple solution to the large water waste will cause the clean water used in the toilet to go to a more necessary and reasonable use.

Hamid Shinwari

Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

Hamid is our CEO and founder of Clear. He is educated as a computer engineer in England and has always been interested in new development. He has no job experience, but he managed to get started with this project with the help of his father who has always been interested in data and technology. Hamid is from a country where outhouses are common amongst all of the population, and they are far from clean and hygienic. This affected Hamid very much.
